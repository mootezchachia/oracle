## Risk Factors
- **Trump diplomatic pivot** — The single biggest risk. Trump has a pattern of maximum pressure → sudden deal (North Korea, China trade). If he announces talks, Yes could spike to 25-40% instantly. However, his prior pivots took months, not days.
